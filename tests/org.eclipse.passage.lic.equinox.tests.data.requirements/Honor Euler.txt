Leonhard Euler 

15 April 1707 – 18 September 1783

a Swiss 
  - mathematician, 
  - physicist, 
  - astronomer, 
  - geographer, 
  - logician and 
  - engineer 

who 
 - founded the study of 
    -- graph theory and 
    -- topology 

 - and made pioneering and influential discoveries in many other branches of mathematics such as 
    -- analytic number theory, 
    -- complex analysis, 
    -- and infinitesimal calculus.
